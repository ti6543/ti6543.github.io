Foil balance simulator

This allows you to look at the effects of changing the setup of your foil

How to use:

  • Adjust the foil setup - wing sizes, fuselage, shims etc.
  • You can change the speed at the bottom. Bigger front foils mean slower stall and top speeds
  • The circle at the bottom shows where the balance point of the foil is - this is where the rider needs to stand

Notes:

  • The front foil is assumed to have the same mounting point, regardless of size. Some brands (e.g. Armstrong, Go Foil etc) are fixed at the rear of the front foil, meaning a bigger foil extends forwards, resulting in effectively a longer fuselage.
  • I have assumed a foil where AoA at -5degrees creates zero lift, for both front wing and stabiliser
  • I have assumed a -2 angle of incidence on the stabiliser
  • The speeds are relative only, and not meant to be an accurate model. Different front wings of the same area can have varying top speeds
