Foil balance simulator

This allows you to look at the effects of changing the setup of your foil

How to use:

  • Adjust the foil setup - wing sizes, fuselage, shims etc.
  • You can change the speed at the bottom. Bigger front foils mean slower stall and top speeds
  • The circle at the bottom shows where the balance point of the foil is - this is where the rider needs to stand
