Apparent Wind

Apparent wind is the wind felt by the rider, as they foil along:

Apparent wind will decrease when going downwind, and increase when gonig upwind.

Going Downwind

Before learning to gybe, we need to learn to go downwind.

This may seem simple, as we can already steer. However, the problem is that as we go further off the wind, the apparent wind reduces.

The issue is that with zero or reversed Apparent Wind, we have nothing to balance against. This is challenging at first, as we're used to having the power of the wing to lean against.

For this reason, before gybing it is essential to be able to go downwind in control, balancing purely on the foil
