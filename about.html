About

I'm Tim, a UK-based enthusiastic wing-foiler, SUP foiler, windsurfer and SUP surfer. I've been windsurfing since the late 90's, and foiling since 2020.

My day job is as a Captain flying A320's around Europe, and this combined with a background in Physics (hopefully) gives me a good understanding of the principles that are key to foiling.

This site is set up to help foilers understand what works and why.
